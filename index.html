Thesis

Design and Development of Textrode based ECG Monitoring System [paper]

Built wearable technology using dry textile electrodes for round-the-clock monitoring and logging of electrocardiogram signals. Heart signals of 0.5mV were collected using a chest strap with textrode patches. The signals were amplified and filtered by designing specialized instrumentation amplifiers and notch filters to extract the QRS complex. The research project was funded by the Center for Materials for Electronics Technology (Govt. of India) and is to be filed for a patent.
